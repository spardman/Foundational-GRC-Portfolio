# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** 2024-05-20  

## 1. Overview
The purpose of this policy is to outline the acceptable use of computer equipment at PureCarbon Cycle Works. Inappropriate use exposes the organization to risks including virus attacks, compromise of network systems, and legal issues.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, contractors, consultants, and other workers at PureCarbon Cycle Works, including all personnel with access to internal networks.

## 3. Policy Statements
*   **System Accounts**: Users are responsible for the security of their passwords and accounts.
*   **Computing Assets**: Laptops and workstations must be secured with password-protected screensavers.
*   **Prohibited Use**: Users shall not engage in illegal activities, harassment, or unauthorized access attempts.

## 4. Compliance & Enforcement
Compliance with this policy is mandatory. Any employee found to have violated this policy may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.
