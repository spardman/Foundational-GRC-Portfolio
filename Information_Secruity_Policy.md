# Information Security Policy (ISP)
**Organization Name:** PureCarbon Cycle Works 
**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Draft  

## 1. Purpose and Scope
This policy defines the high-level principles and objectives for information security management within the organization. It applies to all employees, contractors, and external parties interacting with our systems.

## 2. Framework Alignment
This policy is mapped to the following control frameworks:
*   **NIST CSF (Cybersecurity Framework)**: Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover.
*   **ISO 27001:2023**: Information Security Management Systems.

## 3. Core Policy Statements
*   **Access Control**: Access to organizational assets must be granted based on the **Principle of Least Privilege**.
*   **Data Classification**: All data must be categorized by its criticality and confidentiality (e.g., Public, Internal, Confidential).
*   **Asset Management**: All organizational assets must be inventoried and assigned a responsible owner.

## 4. Roles and Responsibilities
*   **Information Security Officer (ISO)**: Responsible for the final approval and periodic review of this document.
*   **Users**: Responsible for adhering to all stated security procedures and reporting anomalies.

## 5. Review and Compliance
This policy shall be reviewed annually or upon significant changes to the risk landscape. Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action.
